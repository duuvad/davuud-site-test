Most brands are built for the launch. They look right on day one and drift the moment the business moves. I build the other kind: brand run as an operating system, where strategy, identity and output stay aligned while the company scales.

That has held from BMW Group boardrooms to founder-led scale-ups. Different sectors, same pattern. The business had outgrown its brand, and the work closed that gap.
